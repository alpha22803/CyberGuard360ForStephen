Determine Your PCI DSS v4.0 SAQ Type: A Guide for Merchants

SAQ Type Description Eligibility Check
SAQ A

This questionnaire is intended for merchants who outsource all cardholder data functions to PCI DSS compliant third-party service providers and have no direct control of the manner in which cardholder data is processed or stored.

SAQ B

Suitable for merchants who use only imprint machines or standalone, dial-out terminals to process card payments, with no electronic cardholder data storage.

SAQ C

Intended for merchants with payment application systems connected to the Internet, without electronic cardholder data storage.

P2PE

Applicable to merchants using hardware payment terminals included in and managed via a validated, PCI SSC-listed Point-to-Point Encryption (P2PE) solution, with no electronic cardholder data storage.
